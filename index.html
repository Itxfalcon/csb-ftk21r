Salsa Verde Enchiladas

Description

These enchiladas are oozing with cheesy goodness! Filled with honey-lime chicken, salsa verde, and Pepper Jack cheese, this recipe is sure to please even the pickiest eater!

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle salsa verde
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 3 cups shredded Pepper Jack cheese
  • 2 cups Monterey Jack cheese

Marinade

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • l teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Cook chicken in the skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 cup water and let simmer for 15-20 minutes. Drain and shred.
  2. Whisk together the marinade ingredients in a bowl, add the chicken to the marinade, and let marinate for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup salsa verde into a 9x13 cooking dish.
  4. Stir the rest of the salsa verde into the shredded chicken.
  5. Assemble the enchiladas by placing some shredded chicken inside a tortilla, and top with both kinds of cheese. Place in the baking dish seam side down.
  6. Pour any remaining marinade over the top of the enchiladas and top with remaining cheese.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
